Overview

The New Range Rover Evoque launch training is a one-day immersive hands-on and driving experience taking place at seven unique metropolitan cities across the U.S. and Canada. Each event will focus on key features and capabilities, new technologies, position within the market segment, brand lineup, and explore the expanding customer base.
